Marie Curie

The Genius of Marie Curie

Here are some facts about her life and achievements:

  • Born: 7 November 1867

  • Died: 4 July 1934 (aged 66)

  • Fields: Physics and chemistry

  • Known for: Pioneering research on radioactivity and discovering polonium and radium

  • Awards: Nobel Prize in Physics, Nobel Prize in Chemistry, and Davy Medal (just to name a few...)

"Life is not easy for any of us. But what of that? We must have perseverance and above all confidence in ourselves. We must believe that we are gifted for something, and that this thing must be attained."
